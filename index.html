About Us

Spazigo is a digital logistics marketplace founded by a team of entrepreneurs and engineers to make freight services inclusive and accessible for MSMEs. Our Vision To enable small traders and MSMEs to move goods with the same power and convenience as large corporations by leveraging tech-driven, shared cargo infrastructure.
