Key Features

Real-time cargo space availability

Spazigo provides real-time information on available cargo space, helping users find and book what they need instantly.

Verified LSP network

The platform ensures a reliable and trustworthy network by only including verified logistics service providers.

Built-in communication and booking management tools

Users can manage their bookings and communicate directly with LSPs through the platform's integrated tools.

Multi-modal booking

The platform allows users to book cargo across various transportation modes, including road, rail, air, and sea, all in one place.

About Us

Spazigo is a digital logistics marketplace founded by a team of entrepreneurs and engineers to make freight services inclusive and accessible for MSMEs. Our mission is to give small businesses the same power and convenience as large corporations, building a transparent and scalable platform often called the "MakeMyTrip for Freight."

Our Vision To enable small traders and MSMEs to move goods with the same power and convenience as large corporations by leveraging tech-driven, shared cargo infrastructure.
